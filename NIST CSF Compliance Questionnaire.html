Heat Map Inputs

Provide the data for a likelihood vs. impact risk heat map.

On a scale of 1–5 (1 = Minimal, 5 = Critical), rate:
If this system governed by this document fails or is misused, how severe are the consequences to people, organization, society, environment, or planet?

On a scale of 1–5 (1 = Very unlikely, 5 = Very likely), rate:
How likely is it that a harmful event (as defined in the impact question above) will occur, given current controls and context?

These two scores (Impact and Likelihood) are sufficient to place this item on a classic N×N risk heat map (X‑axis = Likelihood, Y‑axis = Impact).
